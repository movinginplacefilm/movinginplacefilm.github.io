ABOUT THE DOCUMENTARY

Young Puerto Ricans share their experiences navigating life on and off the island amidst its ongoing crisis and diaspora. They unpack the complexities of Puerto Rican identity and loyalty, especially in the devastating wake of Hurricane María.

Coming Fall 2018.
